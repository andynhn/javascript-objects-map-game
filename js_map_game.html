Use the console to make the player move "north", "south", "east", or "west" (e.g. move('north'))

Type "player.location" to show player's current location on the map.

Instructions:

Deliver the honey to the appropriate home. The location is already listed in your console.

Pick up the honey at the bees. To pick up, call the pickUp() function while at the bees location.

To drop off the honey, call the drop() function while at the home that needs the honey.

Good luck!
